About me:

As an artist and generally creative individual, I am absolutely fascinated by the many ways software fosters creativity and human connection. In particular, I am interested in crafting fun, easy-to-use, and fully accessible user experiences.

Before coding my time was split between being outside, working as a professional outdoors guide in Colorado and Tennessee, and staring at a screen, making silly little animations for my friends. I guess you could say not much has changed, as I still love being outside and I still spend countless hours staring at my screen, bringing the same care and attention to detail to my software applications as I do to my art. Besides animating, my hobbies include hiking, snowboarding, baking sourdough bread, and playing D&D.

As a full stack software developer I have enjoyed using Javascript, Python, React/Redux, PostgreSQL, Express, Flask, HTML5, CSS3, and Node.js in my projects. I consider myself a life-long learner and look forward to adding other languages and frameworks to my wheelhouse.
