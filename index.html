About me:

Hello, I'm Angie! Software engineer with a background in digital media production, risk management, and baked goods. I first became interested in software while earning my B.F.A. in Art Technology from the University of Oklahoma, which I completed in 2018. While in school I specialized in interactive video installations and animation, additionally studying DSLR photography, video editing, and graphic design. I have extensive experience working with the Adobe Creative Suite, in particular Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, and Lightroom.

As a software engineer I've enjoyed using React, Redux, Express.js, Sequelize, Flask, SQLAlchemy, PostgreSQL, Heroku, Git, Docker, and Node.js. I consider myself a life-long learner and look forward to adding more technologies to my wheelhouse.

When I'm not coding you can usually find me hiking through Colorado's beautiful 14rs, experimenting with my sourdough starter, or working on silly animations.
