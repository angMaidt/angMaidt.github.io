About me:

Hey there, I'm Angie! I'm a software developer with a background in the digital arts, the kitchen, and the river. Before I dropped everything to study software full-time, I worked as a baker during the winter season and a river guide during the summer. Ask me how working in a kitchen trained me to consider time, space, and cost efficiency in my everyday routine! Ask me about my time as a river guide to hear swashbuckling tales of adventure (and risk management). When I'm not coding you can usually find me hiking through Colorado's beautiful 14rs, experimenting with my sourdough starter, or working on silly animations.

--Elementary proficiency with Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effects, Cinema 4D, Maya, and MakerBot software.
